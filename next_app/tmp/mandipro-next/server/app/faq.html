Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MandiGrow — India's #1 mandi ERP software for fruits and vegetable merchants.

What is the best mandi ERP software in India?+

MandiGrow is widely considered the best mandi ERP software in India because it is built specifically for fruits and vegetable merchants, commission agents and wholesale traders. Unlike generic tools, it handles lots, crates, weight, commission, market fees and khata natively in both Hindi and English.

Is MandiGrow better than Tally for fruits and vegetable traders?+

Yes. Tally is a general accounting tool, while MandiGrow is a complete fruits and vegetable ERP. You get everything Tally offers — ledgers, GST, daybook — plus mandi-specific features like commission auto-calculation, lot tracking, wastage and farmer settlements.

Can MandiGrow handle commission agent accounts?+

Absolutely. MandiGrow is a full commission agent software for mandi businesses. It auto-calculates commission, market fees and hamali for every sale, posts entries to khatas instantly, and generates party-wise settlement reports in one click.

Does MandiGrow support GST billing for vegetable wholesalers?+

Yes. MandiGrow generates GST-compliant invoices, supports B2B and B2C billing, and is ready for e-invoicing. You can file GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B faster because all your sales and purchase data is already organized.

Is MandiGrow available in Hindi and regional languages?+

Yes. MandiGrow is fully multilingual and supports Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam and Urdu. The entire app, bills, prints and reports are available in every supported language. Switch with one tap.

Does MandiGrow work as a mandi khata software?+

Yes. MandiGrow is a modern mandi khata software that replaces paper bahis with a fast, mobile-friendly digital khata. Track party balances, advances, payments and credits in real time.

Can I use MandiGrow on a mobile phone?+

Yes. MandiGrow runs on Android phones and on the desktop web — using the same account and the same data. You can take orders at the mandi gate and review reports from home.

Is MandiGrow a good Zoho Books alternative for vegetable traders?+

Yes. MandiGrow is the leading Zoho alternative for vegetable traders because Zoho was built for service businesses, while MandiGrow is built for fruits and vegetable trade — including stock, lots, commission and khata.

How long does it take to set up MandiGrow?+
