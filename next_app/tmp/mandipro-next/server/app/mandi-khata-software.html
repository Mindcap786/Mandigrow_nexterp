Mandi Khata Software — FAQ

What is mandi khata software?

Mandi khata software is a digital ledger built specifically for mandi traders and commission agents. It replaces paper bahis with a real-time, party-wise khata that updates the moment you record a sale, purchase, advance or payment.

How is MandiGrow khata different from a regular khata book app?

Generic khata apps only record udhar. MandiGrow is a full mandi ledger system: it links every khata entry to the underlying sale, purchase, commission and GST entry — so your books are always tally-clean and audit-ready.

Can I see all my party balances at once?

Yes. The party balances screen shows every farmer and buyer with their current outstanding, advance, last transaction date and total turnover — sortable and filterable.

Does the khata work offline?

Yes — MandiGrow caches your data locally and syncs as soon as the connection is back. You can record entries from the mandi gate even with patchy mobile data.

Can I share a party statement on WhatsApp?

Yes. Generate any party statement, export as PDF, and share on WhatsApp directly from the app. Bilingual: Hindi or English.
